ABOUT ME

I'm a Pharmacist (to-be), wanna-be coder and avid unicorn lover

I'm a final year Pharmacy Undergraduate studying at the National University of Singapore. Besides memorising drug names and studying pharmacology, what I love about the Pharmacy profession is that we can empower patients by imparting our knowledge to them, allowing them to take charge of their own healthcare. My (self-proclaimed) niche in this industry is that I am a coding pharmacist! I started off learning Python (for an NUS module) and since then, I've only just begun to discover the capabilities that coding has in store.

Silas New (yes, New is my surname)


Besides Pharmacy, I also love cooking and baking! From local delights like curry chicken to bougie avocado toasts, I'm always down to try out new recipes (and force people to try them). Having said that, I tend to enjoy baking more! My favourites are strawberry shortcake cupcakes, although I also do bake simple brownies and cookies from time to time. I was really tempted to jump on the sourdough bandwagon during the Circuit Breaker but... bread is something I have yet to bake successfully (stay tuned).

Up until recently, I was leading an acapella group in Tembusu College (called the Tembusu Treblemakers - yes, we came up with that name before Pitch Perfect). The highlight of my singing career was getting to sing at the same event as Nathan Hartono and The Sam Willows. Oh, and I also play the violin and create watercolour paintings!
